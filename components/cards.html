Cards

I decided to do something different here and played around with neumophism for the cards. When the cursor hovers over the cards, they expand and pop up. While they are being clicked, they depress and appear to sink into the background.

Accessibility Considerations

Neomorphism is tricky when it comes to accessibility due to contrast issues which can occur through the design. I attempted to mitigate this by using a dark shadow instead of a light one in order to make the cards pop out more from the background. I used the No Coffee Vision Simulator browser plugin to test out how the cards might look to users with many different types of visual impairments (ex: low contrast, color deficiencies, etc). Tests with this tool show that these neomorphic cards passed for those with vision impairments.



Use Cases

  • Can also be used for the "Projects" page. Grid view.
