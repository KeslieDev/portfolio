Caono Design System

About this Design System

This website shows the design elements that went into building this portfolio. This design system was developed in Figma and then transported into this website using the Bootstrap CSS Framework. Design accessibility was verified via the use of multiple web tools referenced within their respective sections.

Please visit the Portfolio section to view my full body work. Thank you!

Accessible

I keep accessibility in mind in all that I do. If there is someone who cannot use a product due to inaccessibility, then it is not finished. I incorporate my lived experiences and the WCAG standards of accessibility to ensure designs are usable by all peoples.


Research-Based

I may not know all the answers, but I will find them. I am always learning, so I am ready to research something I may not know yet in order to push a design further.


Inventive

I strive to be different, and in doing so I try to come up with novel ways of approaching things. Often this means taking a (calculated) risk on something that may not have been done before.


Empathetic

Be kind, always.
